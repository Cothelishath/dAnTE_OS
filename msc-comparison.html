Recommendation
Primary Recommendation
University of Strathclyde — MSc Engineering Management (Online)
Strathclyde scores highest overall because it is the only institution that combines strong curriculum match (7/8), full online delivery, international recognition, and continued work capability simultaneously. The online mode means Dante Craft continues operating, CGSL contract continues, trading continues. The degree is internationally credible and directly applicable to both the engineering career and the dAnTE enterprise build. Cost is the lowest of the UK options.

Cranfield is the better programme academically — 8/8 curriculum match, strongest engineering management reputation in the UK — but it requires full-time campus attendance which disrupts everything else. If Paul reaches a financial position where full-time study is viable, Cranfield is the aspirational choice.

UNIPORT remains a legitimate fallback — no relocation, continued earnings, local network — but the curriculum needs verification before it can be properly scored. If verified at 6+/8, it becomes a serious cost-effective option for the right season.
