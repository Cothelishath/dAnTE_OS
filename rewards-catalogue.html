Daily Activity Ranks
What each rank earns · Suggestions, not prescriptions · Pick what fits the moment
Rewards here are not purchases to be forced. They are suggestions — a menu of things you already enjoy or want, matched to what you have earned. Some are free. Some cost something. None require spending money you don't have. The point is to name the milestone and attach something to it that makes reaching it feel like something worth reaching for.
🌱
Bronze Day
100+ AP · The covenant held
You showed up. The minimum was honoured. Bronze days are the bedrock — they compound into silver weeks and gold months. This deserves acknowledgement, not indifference.
Free
A deliberately good cup of coffee or tea — made properly, not rushed. Sit with it. 10 minutes of something beautiful on YouTube — architecture, travel, design. A walk that goes nowhere particular. Permission to enjoy the evening without guilt.
Small treat
A favourite snack or drink — something you'd normally think twice about. A good meal instead of a convenient one. A cold Guinness or a spirit you like, enjoyed slowly. One track from a playlist you love, played loud.
Experience
An episode of something you're watching — guilt-free, not a compromise. A meal at a sit-down spot instead of eating in. Call someone you've been meaning to call.
🌿
Silver Day
160+ AP · Building territory
More than the minimum. You moved into building territory — the day had real traction. Silver days are where the work starts to feel like yours. Something slightly more is earned.
Free
An hour of pure leisure — a sport you're watching, a film you've wanted to see, an evening of music and doing nothing in particular. Fully guilt-free because it was earned. Pinterest or dreaming session with no time pressure.
Small spend
A restaurant meal — sit-down, good atmosphere, take your time. A new book added to the reading list. A bottle of something good for the apartment. A quality item for the daily routine — good soap, a candle, something small that makes the space better.
Experience
Cinema visit — pick something you've wanted to see. A long walk somewhere new in the city. An evening out — a bar with a good atmosphere, somewhere to sit and observe people and life.
🌳
Gold Day
210+ AP · A genuinely full day
Full engagement across all categories. A genuinely good day — the kind that, replicated enough times, changes the trajectory. Gold days deserve something deliberate, not incidental.
Free
Document the day. Write what fired, what felt good, what you want to remember. This is the record that matters more than any purchase. A Gold Day entry in the Living Notes of the Identity Papers.
Experience
A proper restaurant experience — somewhere with atmosphere, not just food. A live sport event if one is available. A film at the cinema with a good pre-film meal. An evening that feels like a celebration rather than a wind-down.
Personal
An item from the wardrobe list — a single well-chosen piece, not a haul. A skincare product you've been researching. A photography tutorial or the first lesson toward a new skill. A book you've been saving for a meaningful moment.
🌲
Legendary Day
270+ AP · Rare and earned
Everything fired. You are reminded today of exactly who you can be when the friction is removed. Legendary days are rare by design — and their reward should match the rarity.
Mark it
Date noted in the Identity Papers. Entry written — not just a log, a reflection on what the day felt like. This day enters the permanent record. You will return to this entry when momentum is low.
Experience
A meal at the best restaurant you can access — the kind you'd take someone to impress them. A full cinema experience — good seat, good film, no rushing. A live event if possible — sport, music, culture. Something that costs real money and produces a real memory.
Personal
A meaningful wardrobe piece — not fast fashion, something that will still matter in 5 years. A watch research session — begin identifying what you actually want. A photography class booking. A quality whisky or spirit to open on a future milestone. Something that says: I am the kind of person who earns this.

Streak Milestones
Consistency is the rarest discipline · Each threshold earned, not given
Streak rewards are cumulative in spirit but discrete in practice — hitting 7 days earns its own reward separately from the 3-day reward. These are character statements, not just metrics. Reward them accordingly.

A full unbroken week. You've reset your baseline. What you've done once, you can do again — and again.

Something from the wardrobe list, a quality grooming product, a book you've been saving, a tool for photography or a new skill. One considered item that reflects the person the 14 days are building. Not a reward for the body — a reward for the identity.

A month of showing up. This is a character statement. You are the kind of person who does this.

A significant wardrobe investment. A quality watch — researched, chosen, owned. Or: a photography course completed. Whatever the 100-day version of Paul wants — he has earned the right to it.
365 days
🌍 The Year
A full year of deliberate living. You are unrecognisable from who you were on day one. This is what a life looks like when it is chosen.
An international experience
A year of compounding earns a year-defining experience. A trip — booked, taken, remembered. A first real travel memory. The kind of thing you describe to people for years. If not international yet: a domestic trip of real significance — somewhere meaningful, done right. The year earns a story.
