The work
Understanding money
well enough to build with it.
You know money comes in and you stretch it. That is survival. This curriculum builds the layer above survival — the understanding of how money works, what it can do, and how to use it deliberately to build the life and institutions you are working toward. Start at the foundation. Do not skip ahead.
Three tiers
Tier I — Foundation
How money actually works.
Before strategy, before investment, before anything — you must understand the fundamentals. How money moves, what it costs to borrow it, what it earns when saved or invested, and what the Nigerian financial system looks like. These four pillars are non-negotiable foundations.
Money Mindset Budgeting & Cash Flow Debt & Interest The Nigerian Financial System
Tier II — Development
How money grows.
Savings, investment, tax, and business finance. How to make money work while you work. How to read a financial statement. How to understand what a business is worth. These pillars are the bridge between surviving and building.
Savings & Emergency Funds Investing Foundations Tax & Obligations Business Finance
Tier III — Integration
How money serves vision.
Wealth building and financial independence are not the end goal — they are the means by which you build, give, and lead freely. These two pillars connect your financial knowledge to the long vision of dAnTE Inc. and the life you are building.
Wealth Building & Independence Money & the Mission
How this works

02
Nigeria-specific layer
Most financial education is American or British. Where relevant, each pillar has a Nigeria-specific note — what the concept looks like in the Nigerian context. Don't import frameworks that don't apply.
03
Apply to real numbers
Every pillar has a practice that touches your actual financial situation — not a hypothetical. The learning is only real when it changes a decision you make with real money.
04
Bring it back
Each pillar ends with questions to bring back here. We talk through what you learned and how it applies to dAnTE Inc., trading, and your personal financial situation.
