The
Declaration
Paul Akpan Bassey · 2026
Read
01
Foundation
By the Grace, Mercy, Favour and Faithfulness of God, and by the Blood of Jesus Christ which has redeemed us, Exceeding Great Fortune is our portion, We flourish on every side.
02
Standing
We can not, We will not fail; We can not, We will not fall, because God is for us, God is with us, God is in us.
03
Inheritance
The lines fall for us in pleasant places, ours is a delightful, goodly inheritance.
04
Harvest
For us, harvesting season meets and overtakes planting season: our lives yield sweet wine and fruit.
05
New Things
Our Father is doing a new thing for us, in us and through us: see it spring forth! He makes for us roads in the wilderness and streams in the wasteland.
06
Open Doors
Great and effectual doors are open to us and in Jesus' Name every adversary is conquered — for He has given us a mouth and wisdom which no adversary can gainsay or resist.
07
Abundance
Therefore in times of famine We are satisfied and overflowing in abundance, and our Father gives us beauty for ashes, the wealth and riches of the heathen for our inheritance, and this day He gives us our daily bread.
08
The Shepherd
All things work together for our good according to the Will of God for us, the Lord is our Shepherd, We lack nothing and we fear no evil for by His Spirit and divine power He has blessed us with all things that pertain to life and godliness — and through Christ who strengthens us we can do all things, and we prosper in all we do because God is with us.
09
Fruit
As trees planted by God and in God, we bring forth and bear fruit in season and out of season.
10
Manifestation
We manifest Greatness and Life abundant, We manifest the Nature and Wisdom of God, and we do greater works. We transcend limitations, boundaries and possibilities.
11
Identity
We are blessed of God, we are marvellously helped, we are co-labourers with God, we are His investment, we are vessels for His purpose.
12
Witness
We are testimonies, we are signs and wonders in the earth, we are carriers of God's glory, we embody God's Life and Faith, we are stewards, beneficiaries and expressions of God's manifold Grace, we are conduit of His power.
13
Partnership
We are partners with His Holy Spirit and we are Witness. Everyday, everywhere, we walk in prepared places and prepared blessings.
14
Triumph
Thanks be to God who causes us to triumph in all things through Christ Jesus. Thanks be to God who teaches us to make wealth, teaches our hand to make war and our fingers to fight.
15
Valiant
Thanks be to God through whom we run through troops, leap over walls, and perform valiantly. In this era, great things continue to happen in our lives of their own accord, and supernatural doors are open to us as we hold fast to God and His promises to us.
Amen.
