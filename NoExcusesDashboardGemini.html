The Master System for Achievement

The 7-Step Goal Setting Process:
  1. Decide exactly what you want.
  2. Write it down (Think on paper).
  3. Set a deadline for your goal.
  4. Make a list of everything you need to do to achieve it.
  5. Organize the list into a plan (Priority and Sequence).
  6. Take action on your plan immediately.
  7. Do something every single day that moves you toward your major goal.

The 10-Goal Exercise

Write down 10 goals you want to achieve in the next 12 months. Ask: "Which one goal, if achieved, would have the greatest positive impact on my life?" Circle that goal and make it your Primary Purpose.
