This curriculum takes you from not knowing what to do, to knowing exactly what to do next, and the next thing after that. Sequenced for where you actually are. Nigeria-specific where it matters. Anchored to the real companies you are building.

Tier I — Foundation

The first steps for Dante Craft specifically — activating the registered entity, understanding what a business actually is, and building the minimum viable structure needed to take a first client or project. This is the most urgent tier.

Teams, governance, and scale.
Teams, leadership, corporate governance, and the transition from a one-person operation to a company with structure. This is also where the relationship between Dante Craft and dAnTE Inc. begins to take formal shape.
Teams & Collaboration Corporate Governance Strategy & Competitive Positioning

The conglomerate horizon.
