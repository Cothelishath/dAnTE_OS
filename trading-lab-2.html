The three modes
▾
INTAKE
Consume material only. Video, article, chapter. Your only job is to stay present and note what feels important. No analysis, no testing, no writing definitions. Just receive. Background audio is fine here.
PROCESSING
Take one concept from intake and work it until you own it. Write the definition in your own words. Answer: under what exact conditions is this true, and when does it break? No new material comes in. This is where ruleset entries are written.
TESTING
Take one processed concept and go find it in charts. Not to trade — to confirm it exists, to see what valid looks like versus invalid, to break it. This is what turns theory into pattern recognition.
Declare your mode before you open anything. That declaration alone removes most of the paralysis — you now know what done looks like.
