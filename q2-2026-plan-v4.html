Non-Negotiable Rules — Lock These In
Only trade with 0.05 lots ($0.50 per trade) until 10 profitable trades are documented
No exceptions. Not one. The strategy must prove itself at the minimum viable stake before any scaling. One undisciplined large lot can end the account.
Every tradeNon-negotiable
Every trade must be documented in the Trading Journal before the next session
No undocumented trades. Entry, setup, confluences, before screenshot, result, lesson. The journal is part of the trade — not optional.
Every trade
Maximum 1 trade per session. Maximum 2 trades per day.
This is not about opportunity — it is about discipline. Overtrading is the primary account killer at this stage. One clear setup, fully validated. That is enough.
Daily limit
If 2 consecutive losses occur in a day — stop trading for that day
Two losses means something is off — market conditions, your state, or your read. The third trade will likely be revenge. Stop.
Kill switch
